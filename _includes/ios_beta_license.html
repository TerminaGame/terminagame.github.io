About the beta program

The Termina for iOS Beta Program is a means of publicly beta-testing the iOS version of Termina, the SpriteKit GUI for the Termina project. The beta program works hand-in-hand with TestFlight as a means of testing and distribution.

Participant Notice

The Termina for iOS Beta Program is limited to 10,000 participants as per Apple's TestFlight limitations. You may need to check regularly for updates or openings if you are unavailable to test at this time.
