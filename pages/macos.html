Termina for macOS

The same dungeons from Termina, now on your Mac

The dungeon game for the terminal now makes its way to the Mac with features and looks so amazing you'd want to play it again. And again.

Endless rooms and fun.

Termina for macOS holds its roots with the original Termina game while using SpriteKit to bring it to life. Enter new rooms, collect items, catch errors, and heal with a new graphical look.
